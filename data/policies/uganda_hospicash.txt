POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name: HospiCash Uganda
Policy Code: TUR-UG-HC-001
Country: Uganda
Product Type: HospiCash
Insurer: Turaco Insurance
Currency: UGX (Ugandan Shilling) / USD equivalent shown in brackets

OVERVIEW
--------
HospiCash Uganda provides a daily cash benefit for each night spent as an
inpatient in a registered Ugandan hospital. Distributed via Airtel Money and
Turaco partner networks across Uganda.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Age: 18 to 65 years at enrollment
- Residency: Must be a Uganda resident
- Enrollment: Via Airtel Money Uganda, Tugende, or other approved partners
- Waiting period: 14 days from activation (no waiting period for accidents)

COVERAGE DETAILS
----------------
Daily Cash Benefit: UGX 30,000 (~$8 USD) per night of inpatient stay
Maximum Benefit per Claim: UGX 300,000 (~$80 USD) — 10 nights maximum
Maximum Benefit per Year: UGX 600,000 (~$160 USD)
Minimum Qualifying Stay: 1 night (inpatient admission required)

COVERED CONDITIONS (Diagnosis Codes)
--------------------------------------
- Malaria (ICD-10: B50–B54) — high prevalence, automatically fast-tracked
- Typhoid Fever (ICD-10: A01.0)
- Pneumonia (ICD-10: J18)
- Tuberculosis (ICD-10: A15–A19)
- Cholera (ICD-10: A00) — specific to Uganda endemic zones
- Acute Gastroenteritis (ICD-10: A09)
- Sickle Cell Crisis (ICD-10: D57)
- Urinary Tract Infection (ICD-10: N39.0)
- Road Traffic Accident injuries (ICD-10: V01–V99)
- Boda-boda (motorcycle) accident injuries
- Maternity complications (ICD-10: O00–O99) — female policyholders only
- Surgical procedures requiring overnight admission

EXCLUSIONS
----------
- Outpatient visits without formal admission
- Cosmetic or elective procedures
- Pre-existing conditions (first 6 months)
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Treatment outside Uganda
- Traditional medicine costs

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
------------------
Submit via WhatsApp:
1. Hospital discharge summary or inpatient receipt
2. Itemized bill from hospital
3. Doctor's diagnosis note (ICD-10 code or diagnosis name required)
4. Policyholder's Turaco policy number
5. National ID (Uganda National ID or valid passport)

PAYOUT
------
- Approved claims paid to Airtel Money or MTN Mobile Money within 3 working days
- Amount = (qualifying nights) x UGX 30,000
- Capped at UGX 300,000 per claim

CLAIM LIMITS SUMMARY
--------------------
Single claim maximum: UGX 300,000 (10 nights)
Annual maximum: UGX 600,000
Per-night benefit: UGX 30,000
Minimum nights: 1
