POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name: Last Expense Kenya
Policy Code: TUR-KE-LE-001
Country: Kenya
Product Type: Last Expense (Funeral Cash Benefit)
Insurer: Turaco Insurance
Currency: KES (Kenyan Shilling) / USD equivalent shown in brackets

OVERVIEW
--------
Last Expense insurance provides a lump-sum cash payment to the beneficiary
upon the death of the policyholder. The benefit is intended to cover funeral
and burial costs, reducing financial burden on the family during bereavement.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Age: 18 to 70 years at enrollment
- Residency: Must be a Kenya resident
- Enrollment: Via approved Turaco partners
- Waiting period: 6 months for natural causes; no waiting period for accidental death

COVERAGE DETAILS
----------------
Death Benefit (Natural Causes): KES 30,000 (~$225 USD)
Death Benefit (Accidental Death): KES 50,000 (~$375 USD)
Repatriation Assistance: Up to KES 10,000 (~$75 USD) for transport of remains within Kenya

COVERED CAUSES OF DEATH
------------------------
- Natural illness (after 6-month waiting period)
- Road traffic accident
- Workplace accident
- Drowning
- Fire-related death
- Any sudden, unexpected cause

EXCLUSIONS
----------
- Suicide or self-inflicted death
- Death resulting from participation in criminal activity
- Death from war or civil unrest
- Death while under the influence of non-prescribed substances

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
------------------
To file a Last Expense claim, the following must be submitted via WhatsApp:
1. Certified copy of Death Certificate (from registrar of births and deaths)
2. Burial permit
3. National ID of the deceased
4. National ID of the beneficiary / claimant
5. Proof of relationship to the deceased (if beneficiary is not spouse)
6. Turaco policy number of the deceased

PAYOUT
------
- Paid to nominated beneficiary via M-PESA or bank transfer within 3 working days
- Lump sum, not per-night benefit

CLAIM LIMITS SUMMARY
--------------------
Natural death benefit: KES 30,000
Accidental death benefit: KES 50,000
Repatriation support: KES 10,000
Claim period: Must be filed within 90 days of death
