POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name: Last Expense Nigeria
Policy Code: TUR-NG-LE-001
Country: Nigeria
Product Type: Last Expense (Funeral Cash Benefit)
Insurer: Turaco Insurance
Currency: NGN (Nigerian Naira) / USD equivalent shown in brackets

OVERVIEW
--------
Last Expense Nigeria provides a lump-sum cash payment to the designated
beneficiary upon death of the policyholder. The payment helps families manage
the high cost of Nigerian burial ceremonies and funeral arrangements.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Age: 18 to 70 years at enrollment
- Residency: Must be a Nigeria resident
- Enrollment: Via approved Turaco Nigeria partners
- Waiting period: 6 months for natural causes; no waiting period for accidents

COVERAGE DETAILS
----------------
Death Benefit (Natural Causes): NGN 100,000 (~$120 USD)
Death Benefit (Accidental Death): NGN 150,000 (~$180 USD)
Repatriation Assistance: Up to NGN 30,000 (~$36 USD) for transport within Nigeria

COVERED CAUSES OF DEATH
------------------------
- Natural illness (post 6-month waiting period)
- Road traffic accident
- Workplace accident
- Drowning
- Fire-related death
- Any sudden, unexpected accidental cause

EXCLUSIONS
----------
- Suicide or self-inflicted death
- Death from participation in criminal activity
- Death from war or terrorism
- Death from substance abuse

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
------------------
Submit via WhatsApp:
1. Certified Death Certificate issued by Nigerian authorities
2. Burial or cremation permit
3. NIN (National Identification Number) or valid ID of deceased
4. Valid ID of beneficiary/claimant
5. Evidence of relationship to deceased if beneficiary is not spouse
6. Turaco policy number of the deceased

PAYOUT
------
- Paid to nominated beneficiary via OPay, bank transfer, or mobile wallet
- Must be filed within 90 days of death
- Processed within 3 working days of complete document submission

CLAIM LIMITS SUMMARY
--------------------
Natural death benefit: NGN 100,000
Accidental death benefit: NGN 150,000
Repatriation support: NGN 30,000
Filing deadline: 90 days from date of death
