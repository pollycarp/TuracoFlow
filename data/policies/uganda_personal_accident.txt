POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name: Personal Accident Uganda
Policy Code: TUR-UG-PA-001
Country: Uganda
Product Type: Personal Accident
Insurer: Turaco Insurance
Currency: UGX (Ugandan Shilling) / USD equivalent shown in brackets

OVERVIEW
--------
Personal Accident insurance provides cash compensation for accidental injuries,
permanent disability, or accidental death. Designed for boda-boda riders,
construction workers, and informal sector workers in Uganda.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Age: 18 to 60 years at enrollment
- Residency: Must be a Uganda resident
- Enrollment: Via Tugende, Airtel Money, or approved Turaco partners
- Waiting period: None — coverage begins immediately upon activation

COVERAGE DETAILS
----------------
Accidental Death Benefit: UGX 1,000,000 (~$270 USD)
Permanent Total Disability: UGX 1,000,000 (~$270 USD)
Permanent Partial Disability: UGX 100,000–500,000 depending on severity
Medical Expense Reimbursement (Accident-related): Up to UGX 200,000 (~$54 USD)
Hospital Cash (Accident-related admission): UGX 30,000/night up to 10 nights

COVERED ACCIDENT TYPES
-----------------------
- Road traffic accidents (boda-boda, matatu, vehicle)
- Workplace accidents (construction, factory, farm)
- Falls resulting in fractures or injuries
- Electrocution accidents
- Burns from fire or explosion
- Drowning (non-suicidal)
- Any sudden, violent, external event causing bodily injury

DISABILITY SCALE
----------------
Loss of both hands or feet: 100% (UGX 1,000,000)
Loss of one hand and one foot: 100% (UGX 1,000,000)
Loss of sight in both eyes: 100% (UGX 1,000,000)
Loss of one leg above knee: 60% (UGX 600,000)
Loss of one arm above elbow: 60% (UGX 600,000)
Loss of one eye: 40% (UGX 400,000)
Loss of thumb: 20% (UGX 200,000)
Loss of index finger: 10% (UGX 100,000)

EXCLUSIONS
----------
- Self-inflicted injuries or suicide attempts
- Injuries from participation in criminal activities
- Injuries while under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Pre-existing physical disabilities
- War, civil unrest, or terrorism
- Extreme sports (mountaineering, skydiving) unless declared at enrollment
- Illness-related conditions (use HospiCash for illness)

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
------------------
Submit via WhatsApp:
1. Police report (for road accidents — P3 form)
2. Hospital medical report describing injuries and treatment
3. Discharge summary
4. Photos of injuries if available (optional but helpful)
5. National ID of claimant
6. Turaco policy number

PAYOUT
------
- Medical reimbursements: within 3 working days
- Disability claims: within 5 working days after assessment
- Death claims: within 5 working days of receiving all documents
- All payments via Airtel Money or MTN Mobile Money

CLAIM LIMITS SUMMARY
--------------------
Accidental death: UGX 1,000,000
Total disability: UGX 1,000,000
Medical reimbursement cap: UGX 200,000
Hospital cash per night: UGX 30,000
Maximum hospital cash per claim: UGX 300,000 (10 nights)
