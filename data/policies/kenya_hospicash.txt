POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name: HospiCash Kenya
Policy Code: TUR-KE-HC-001
Country: Kenya
Product Type: HospiCash
Insurer: Turaco Insurance
Currency: KES (Kenyan Shilling) / USD equivalent shown in brackets

OVERVIEW
--------
HospiCash provides a daily cash benefit for every night a policyholder spends
as an inpatient in a registered hospital. The benefit is paid regardless of
actual medical bills, allowing the customer to cover lost income, transport,
or food for their family.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Age: 18 to 65 years at enrollment
- Residency: Must be a Kenya resident
- Enrollment: Via approved Turaco partners (M-KOPA, Safaricom, Shell)
- Waiting period: 14 days from policy activation (no waiting period for accidents)

COVERAGE DETAILS
----------------
Daily Cash Benefit: KES 1,000 (~$7.50 USD) per night of inpatient stay
Maximum Benefit per Claim: KES 10,000 (~$75 USD) — 10 nights maximum
Maximum Benefit per Year: KES 20,000 (~$150 USD)
Minimum Qualifying Stay: 1 night (must be admitted, not just outpatient)

COVERED CONDITIONS (Diagnosis Codes)
--------------------------------------
The following conditions qualify for HospiCash if treatment requires inpatient admission:
- Malaria (ICD-10: B50–B54)
- Typhoid Fever (ICD-10: A01.0)
- Pneumonia (ICD-10: J18)
- Tuberculosis (ICD-10: A15–A19)
- Acute Gastroenteritis / Diarrhoea (ICD-10: A09)
- Urinary Tract Infection (ICD-10: N39.0)
- Hypertensive Crisis (ICD-10: I10)
- Diabetic Emergency (ICD-10: E11)
- Appendicitis (ICD-10: K37)
- Road Traffic Accident injuries (ICD-10: V01–V99)
- Maternity complications (ICD-10: O00–O99) — female policyholders only
- Surgical procedures requiring overnight admission

EXCLUSIONS
----------
- Outpatient visits (no overnight stay)
- Cosmetic or elective procedures not medically necessary
- Pre-existing conditions diagnosed before enrollment (first 6 months)
- Self-inflicted injuries or substance abuse
- Treatment outside Kenya
- Experimental treatments not approved by Kenya Medical Board

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
------------------
To file a claim, the following documents must be submitted via WhatsApp:
1. Hospital discharge summary or admission receipt
2. Receipt showing total charges and itemized bill
3. Diagnosis note from attending doctor (must include ICD-10 code or diagnosis name)
4. Patient's Turaco policy number
5. National ID or passport copy of policyholder

PAYOUT
------
- Approved claims paid to M-PESA within 3 working days
- Amount = (Number of qualifying nights) x KES 1,000
- Capped at KES 10,000 per single claim event

CLAIM LIMITS SUMMARY
--------------------
Single claim maximum: KES 10,000 (10 nights)
Annual maximum: KES 20,000
Per-night benefit: KES 1,000
Minimum nights for claim: 1
