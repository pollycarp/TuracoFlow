POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name: HospiCash Nigeria
Policy Code: TUR-NG-HC-001
Country: Nigeria
Product Type: HospiCash
Insurer: Turaco Insurance
Currency: NGN (Nigerian Naira) / USD equivalent shown in brackets

OVERVIEW
--------
HospiCash Nigeria provides a daily cash benefit for inpatient hospital stays.
Designed for low-income earners across Lagos, Abuja, Kano, Port Harcourt, and
other major Nigerian cities, distributed through NIBSS Instant Pay and partner
mobile wallets.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Age: 18 to 65 years at enrollment
- Residency: Must be a Nigeria resident
- Enrollment: Via approved Turaco Nigeria partners
- Waiting period: 14 days from activation (no waiting period for accidents)

COVERAGE DETAILS
----------------
Daily Cash Benefit: NGN 5,000 (~$6 USD) per night of inpatient stay
Maximum Benefit per Claim: NGN 50,000 (~$60 USD) — 10 nights maximum
Maximum Benefit per Year: NGN 100,000 (~$120 USD)
Minimum Qualifying Stay: 1 night (formal inpatient admission required)

COVERED CONDITIONS (Diagnosis Codes)
--------------------------------------
- Malaria (ICD-10: B50–B54)
- Typhoid Fever (ICD-10: A01.0)
- Pneumonia (ICD-10: J18)
- Tuberculosis (ICD-10: A15–A19)
- Sickle Cell Crisis (ICD-10: D57) — high prevalence in Nigeria
- Meningitis (ICD-10: G03)
- Hypertensive Emergency (ICD-10: I10)
- Diabetic Emergency (ICD-10: E11)
- Acute Gastroenteritis (ICD-10: A09)
- Urinary Tract Infection requiring admission (ICD-10: N39.0)
- Road Traffic Accident injuries (ICD-10: V01–V99)
- Maternity complications (ICD-10: O00–O99) — female policyholders only
- Surgical procedures requiring overnight admission

EXCLUSIONS
----------
- Outpatient consultations without overnight admission
- Cosmetic procedures
- Pre-existing conditions (first 6 months)
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Treatment outside Nigeria
- Traditional / herbal medicine costs

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS
------------------
Submit via WhatsApp:
1. Hospital discharge summary or inpatient admission receipt
2. Itemized hospital bill
3. Doctor's note with diagnosis (ICD-10 code or diagnosis name)
4. Policyholder's Turaco policy number
5. Valid ID (NIN, Driver's Licence, International Passport, or Voter's Card)

PAYOUT
------
- Approved claims paid to OPay, Kuda Bank, or bank account within 3 working days
- Amount = (qualifying nights) x NGN 5,000
- Capped at NGN 50,000 per single claim event

CLAIM LIMITS SUMMARY
--------------------
Single claim maximum: NGN 50,000 (10 nights)
Annual maximum: NGN 100,000
Per-night benefit: NGN 5,000
Minimum nights: 1
